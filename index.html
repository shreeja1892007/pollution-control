Pollution Control

Protect Nature, Protect Yourself

What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into the environment. It affects air, water, soil, and all living beings. Controlling pollution is essential to maintain a healthy planet.

Ways to Control Pollution

Reduce Air Pollution

Use public transport, carpool, avoid burning garbage, and plant trees.

Control Water Pollution

Avoid dumping waste in rivers, use eco-friendly detergents, and treat sewage properly.

Reduce Land Pollution

Recycle plastic, compost organic waste, and reduce single-use products.

Control Noise Pollution

Avoid loudspeakers at night, maintain vehicles properly, and use sound-absorbing materials.
